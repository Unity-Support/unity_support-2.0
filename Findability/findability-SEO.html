SEO Best Practices

"Unlocking Visibility, One Search at a Time."

See our About Us page to read how we do this at Unity Support

In the realm of SEO, several foundational practices contribute significantly to a website's visibility and ranking. Optimizing meta descriptions and title tags; concise, descriptive tags enhance click-through rates.

Employing structured data markup aids search engines in understanding and presenting content more effectively in results.

Regularly updating and repurposing existing content keeps websites relevant and encourages search engine crawlers to revisit.

Furthermore, optimizing images with descriptive file names and alt tags contributes to better accessibility and improved search engine indexing.

Keyword Research

  • Identify relevant keywords related to your content and audience.

Content Quality

  • Create high-quality, relevant, and valuable content that addresses user intent.
  • Focus on creating content that is informative, engaging, and solves the user's query.

Page Speed

  • Optimize website loading times to improve user experience and search rankings.
  • Optimal formatted images and filetypes.

User Experience (UX)

  • Prioritise a positive user experience with an intuitive interface and easy navigation.
