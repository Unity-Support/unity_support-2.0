Unity Support - Findability & Accesibility

Welcome to Unity Support - Your NDIS Services Experts in Perth Metro!

At Unity Support, we're dedicated to bridging service gaps for NDIS participants in Perth Metro. As a registered NDIS provider, we offer specialized Psychosocial Recovery Coaching and Level 2 Support Coordination, ensuring tailored, client-focused care for every individual.

Our founder, Sarah Roczniak, brings a wealth of experience and a deep understanding of mental health, backed by a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology, Criminology & Justice.

With over a decade of empowering individuals with disabilities, Sarah leads our team in providing personalized services to help you achieve your goals.

Discover how Unity Support delivers unwavering commitment and expertise to ensure no participant is left without the vital support they deserve.
